What the heck do you want to do with this weather forecast app?

START
    Welcome page: choose 3/5/7 day forecast
        - click button for day you want -> forecast page with day window available
    Forecast page: show daily forecast as cards, local news style
        - Click on a card -> zoom in view of that day's forecast
        - Link to get back to the Welcome/Change Days Page
    Zoomed in to single Day Forecast Focus
        - Dismiss zoom to go back to forecast page.
